Flight Delay Prediction using Machine Learning


Customer satisfaction with airlines and airport performance is considerably influenced by how many flights are delayed. My passion for the airline industry has led me to do my capstone project at General Assembly with a dataset that is composed of domestic flights within the United States. My dataset ( comprising 26 consecutive months of airline performance, 12 million records, 17 airlines and 346 flight destinations) has been taken from the United States government agency website that provides the air traffic delay statistics in the U.S. The U.S. Department of Transportation's (DOT) Bureau of Transportation Statistics (BTS) performance of domestic flights operated by large air carriers. I tested five machine learning models namely Logistic Regression, AdaBoost, Random Forest, K-Nearest Neighbors tand Neural nets. The Random Forest and neural network algorithms were the best performers.
